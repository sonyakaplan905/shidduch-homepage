Our Story

Why We Started This

The Thornhill Shidduch Centre began with a simple observation: Make it make Sense. Too many resumes were getting lost, too many families were unsure where to turn, and too many singles and parents felt like they were figuring it out alone. This project exists to change that — to give Thornhill's singles and their families a trusted, place to register once, and be supported along the way. Our goal is simple — to serve our own community's singles and families with the care, organization, and dignity this process deserves.
