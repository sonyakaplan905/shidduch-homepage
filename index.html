Our Story

Why We Started This

The Thornhill Shidduch Centre began with a simple observation: singles and families in our own community needed a more organized, more caring way to navigate the shidduch process. Too many resumes were getting lost, too many families were unsure where to turn, and too many singles felt like they were figuring it out alone. This project exists to change that — to give Thornhill's singles and their families a trusted, well-run home base: a place to register once, be taken seriously, and be supported with real resources along the way. Our goal is simple — to serve our own community's singles and families with the care, organization, and dignity this process deserves.
