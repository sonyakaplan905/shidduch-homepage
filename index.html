Our Story

Why We Started This

The Thornhill Shidduch Centre was born from a simple idea: every single deserves the opportunity to be known, included, and supported.

Created after recognizing a need within our own community, the Centre provides practical resources, Torah guidance, and a community registry for Thornhill singles. By helping maintain current information and making it easy for individuals to update their profiles, we hope to support families, assist shadchanim, and create more opportunities for meaningful connections.
